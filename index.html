The Eiffel Tower, or in French, la tour Eiffel, is known as the symbol of capital of France, Paris. The construction of the Tower took two years and it was finally done for opening in March of 1889.


Did you know?

  • The original height of the Eiffel Tower was 300m.
  • The Eiffel Tower was the tallest building in the world until 1930.
  • The tower was supposed to be taken down after 20 years.
